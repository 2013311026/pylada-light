Pylada-light documentation
==========================

Pylada-light is a python framework for high throughput computational materials science.
It is a simplified version of the original :py:mod:`pylada`, which itself came from a tool called :py:mod:`lada`.
These tools were developed primarily at the United States Department of Energy's
National Renewable Energy Laboratory.
